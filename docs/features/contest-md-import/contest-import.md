## entry_method_en

📝 To enter:

1. Buy a minimum of **RM30** worth of any participating Dutch Lady products in a single receipt.
2. Snap a clear picture of the receipt (Proof of Purchase).
3. Send the picture and your details (Full Name, MyKad/MyPR Number) via WhatsApp to **012-662 2186**.

### Multiple Entries

- For every **RM30** purchased in a single receipt, **ONE (01)** serial number will be allocated. (e.g., RM60 = 2 serial numbers, RM90 = 3 serial numbers).
- Each Proof of Purchase is eligible for one submission only. Multiple entries by the same participant with different receipts are allowed.
- Retain the original receipt for verification.

## winners_selection_method_en

🥇 Winners are selected based on the serialized number of qualified entries divided by the allocated prizes.

- **Grand Prize**: Total eligible entries divided by 2.
- **First Prize**: Total eligible entries minus Grand Prize winner's entries, divided by 4.
- **Weekly Prize**: Total eligible weekly entries divided by 8.

The winning number is rounded down to the nearest single digit if there's a decimal, and the multiples of that serial number will be selected as winners.

Shortlisted winners will be contacted via WhatsApp and must answer a simple true/false question correctly within **two (2) days** to win: _"Dutch Lady Milk Industries Berhad’s purpose is to nourish Malaysians with quality dairy nutrition to help them move forward in life."_

## winners_selection_method_ms

🥇 Pemenang dipilih berdasarkan nombor siri penyertaan yang layak dibahagikan dengan hadiah yang diperuntukkan.

- **Hadiah Utama**: Jumlah penyertaan yang layak dibahagikan dengan 2.
- **Hadiah Pertama**: Jumlah penyertaan yang layak tolak penyertaan pemenang Hadiah Utama, dibahagikan dengan 4.
- **Hadiah Mingguan**: Jumlah penyertaan mingguan yang layak dibahagikan dengan 8.

Nombor kemenangan akan dibundarkan ke bawah jika terdapat nilai perpuluhan, dan gandaan nombor siri tersebut akan dipilih sebagai pemenang.

Pemenang yang disenarai pendek akan dihubungi melalui WhatsApp dan mesti menjawab soalan betul/salah yang mudah dengan tepat dalam masa **dua (2) hari** untuk memenangi hadiah: _"Tujuan Dutch Lady Milk Industries Berhad adalah untuk menyuburkan rakyat Malaysia dengan nutrisi tenusu berkualiti untuk membantu mereka maju dalam kehidupan."_
